### **Introduction**

---

### **Objectives**
1. To compare the parcentage of total accidents by `month` and observe if there is any noticeable seasonal pattern
1. To break down the frequency of the accident down into:
    - `days` of the week
    - `hours` of the week
1. To determine when accidents occurs most frequently based on:
    - day of the week
    - hour of the week
1. To check if there were more accidents during weekdays than weekends
